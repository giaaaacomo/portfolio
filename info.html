WHOAMI
Giacomo, creative person from Italy with a minimal approach but often not-so-minimal results. BA in new media arts (Urbino fine arts academy) and MA in interaction and experience design, I like to design experiences with an interdisciplinary approach, trying not to focus solely on the aesthetics but rather with a deep connection to the meaning of things, with a strong research-based approach. I like experimenting with new (and sometimes non conventional) technologies, and don't like doing what's not necessary.
